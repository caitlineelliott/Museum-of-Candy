ABOUT

Museum of Candy transforms concepts and dreams into spaces that provoke imagination and creativity. MOC is designed to be a culturally inclusive environment and community, inspiring human connection and through the universal power of candy. Museum of Candy is a Figure8 brand.

WHO WE ARE

We believe in creating beautiful and shareable environments that foster IRL interaction and URL connections, providing fun, multi-sensorial expressions of ice cream that cater to the appetites of our generation. Now currently stationed in San Francisco and New York City, MOC is just getting started.

WHAT'S INSIDE?

MOC San Francisco is proud to present 13 exhibition spaces with immersive and innovative concepts. Ever-changing with seasonal themes, our fan favorites include the Oh Yeah Room, Celestial Subway, Rainbow Tunnel and of course, the Sprinkle Pool! Stay tuned here for any new concepts!
